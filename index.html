Our Mission. Togather as a community.

The organization’s mission is to foster meaningful connections between local youth and elders, including seniors from diverse cultural backgrounds who have recently arrived in Canada. Through this initiative, our goal is to cultivate compassionate and inclusive communities. We are dedicated to connecting youth supporting the integration of these seniors into Canadian society by providing a wide range of supportive programs, including organizing and coordinating volunteer activities, events, and initiatives tailored to their specific needs. Collaborating with various organizations, our initiatives encompass offering companionship, organizing workshops, clubs, and engaging in activities such as literacy programs, computer application training, handcrafts, sewing, dancing, singing, and culturally relevant field trips. We believe in the importance of providing convenience and support for the elderly, enhancing their sense of belonging and happiness. Elderly individuals who are not lonely and experience a sense of happiness are often those who maintain good health.

Interconnected Generations. Through Shared Experience

Furthermore, our organization will emphasize the creation of opportunities for seniors to engage socially with both younger generations and their peers. This focus on social interaction aims to instill a sense of purpose, meaning, and belonging within the community. Our approach involves facilitating opportunities for seniors to share valuable life experiences, skills and languages with the youth, thus contributing to the community's well-being and growth.

Benefits to Youth. Empowering Youth through Senior Expertise.

• Experience Inheritance: By sharing experiences and knowledge in specific fields, the elderly provide valuable wisdom to the younger generation, helping them better understand and cope with various life challenges.

• Career Guidance: Leveraging their unique backgrounds and professional experience, the elderly can act as career mentors for young people, offering guidance on specific industries, career paths, and workplace challenges, aiding the younger generation in better planning their careers.

• Skill Development: Utilizing their professional skills, such as languages, painting, cooking, etc., the elderly can provide targeted training to young people, enhancing their skills in specific areas.

• Building Social Networks: Through interaction with the elderly, young people can expand their social networks, connecting with individuals from different age groups. This helps broaden their perspectives and provides additional interpersonal resources.

• Psychological Support: Having likely faced various challenges in their life journeys, the elderly, through sharing these experiences, offer psychological support and encouragement to young people, assisting them in better navigating life's uncertainties.

Empowering Tomorrow's Leaders. .

• Accumulation of Experience: Through interactions with the elderly, young people can gain valuable wisdom and experience from their rich life experiences, fostering personal growth and development.

• Interpersonal Relationships: By connecting with the elderly, young people expand their social networks, cultivating a broader circle of connections that can contribute to more opportunities in both professional and personal life.

• Sense of Social Responsibility: Involvement in serving the elderly nurtures a sense of social responsibility and empathy in young individuals, making them more attuned to the overall well-being of society.

• Skill Development: Providing services for the elderly offers young people the opportunity to learn and develop various skills such as communication, organization, and collaboration, which are crucial for their future careers.

Benefits to Seniors. Sharing, Supporting, Thriving.

• Social Activities: The companionship and service provided by young people enrich the social lives of the elderly, alleviating potential negative impacts that may arise from loneliness.

• Emotional Support: The care and assistance from young individuals offer emotional support to the elderly in their daily lives, contributing to maintaining a positive mindset and overall quality of life.

• Skill Inheritance: The elderly have the opportunity to pass on their skills and knowledge to the younger generation, promoting the inheritance of skills and the preservation of cultural traditions.

• Participation in Activities: The involvement of young people can help the elderly participate more actively in community activities, enhancing their sense of well-being.

• Self-value Fulfillment: While assisting young people, the elderly also find a sense of purpose, maintain a positive spirit, contribute to their own health, and make meaningful contributions to society. This intergenerational assistance not only promotes individual well-being but also fosters a more harmonious and fulfilling societal environment.

Benefits to Society. Building Community Bonds.

• Intergenerational Communication: Promoting cross-generational communication between young and old contributes to the construction of more harmonious interpersonal relationships and community environments.

• Social Cohesion: Through intergenerational mutual assistance activities, social cohesion is strengthened, creating a more vibrant and cohesive sense of community.

• Optimization of Resources: By harnessing the vitality of the young and the experience of the elderly, society can more effectively optimize resources, fostering mutual benefits across different age groups.

Benefit for all. Generational Synergy.

The elderly contribute their experience and skills to provide various support for young people, such as serving as mentors or offering assistance based on their background, experiences and expertise. This brings benefits to themselves, society, and the younger generation many different ways.
